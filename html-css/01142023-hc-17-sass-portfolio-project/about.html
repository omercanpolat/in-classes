BIO

I graduated from the Department of Industrial Engineering at the Air Force Academy in 2004. As a first-lieutenant, I worked in junior and mid-level management positions (plan officer, section supervisor and branch manager positions) in various departments of human resources departments (personnel branch directorates) in air force jet bases Izmir and Ankara provinces. Between 2008 and 2010, I completed my master of science degree in "Manpower Systems Analysis" at Naval Postgraduate School in the USA. After 2010, I worked as a project officer/interviewer in the planning of recruiting cadets to air force officers, non-commissioned officers and civil servants. As a manpower planning officer, I followed up the production, development and implementation of the officers', non-commissioned officers' and civil servants' 30/10-year manpower plans by using statistical analysis processes (with the help of statistical programs such as SAP, STATA, Arena). I worked in the units of the TURAF Personnel Directorate such as professional development and career planning, continuing education and courses, registration/appointment/personnel rights, and coordinated meetings on issues related to my roles. I attended in international conferences and symposiums held in NATO member countries and other countries such as Australia, Sweden and Switzerland on topics such as "national security, human resources management in the armed forces" and made presentations on various topics. I worked as an organizer, planner, participant and presenter in the organization of national/international symposiums and conferences held throughout the Turkish Air Forces (TURAF). I worked as a project officer in the follow-up of contracted/active duty officer, non-commissioned officers' recruitment and contract renewal/termination procedures, the creation of recruiting commissions, conducting interviews and monitoring the judicial and administrative processes. Within the scope of the Life-long Education Model, I participated in various courses, trainings, seminars and symposiums given for professional development in the field of human resources. Finally, I was continuing my pilot training in Izmir, the 2nd Main Jet Base Command. On July 8, 2018, I retired from the Air Force. I am currently taking courses from Clarusway Bootcamp for Full-Stack Developer. I am good at HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React. I want to continue and develop my career in IT companies.
